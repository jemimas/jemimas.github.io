About Us

Jemima's Bakery is a family-owned business based in Sangotedo, Lagos, Nigeria. We believe that food should be both nutritious and delicious so we search far & wide for the highest quality ingredients: fresh eggs, premium milk, sweet butter, refined flour, gourmet salt, & more. All combined to the exact specifications of closely-held recipes and baked to perfection.

We make our baked goods with love & great care. Try us and taste the difference.

Our Breads

From traditional sliced bread to specialty breads and rolls, we produce a variety of always delicious bread products.

Premium Sliced White Bread

Perfect for sandwiches, toast, and more, our premium sliced white bread is a classic, filled with goodness and sliced for your convenience. Classic and convenient.

Classic Bread Loaves

Available unsliced in several sizes, you're free to determine the appropriate serving for your appetite. Untouched by our gentle slicing process, the texture of these loaves is simply divine. Simple, yet delicious.

Exotic Fruit Bread

Filled with a variety of exotic fruits, including plump currants and juicy sultanas, our fruit bread is a tasty, richly flavoured blend that's sure to delight the tongue. Exotic and delectable.

Tropical Coconut Bread

With a subtle sweetness and a taste of the tropics, our coconut bread provides familiar flavor enriched by real, shredded coconut. Delicious served alongside a meal of rice and plantains, or enjoy it with your morning cup of coffee. Subtle, yet scrumptious.

Sweet Rolls

Starting with buttery dough and a careful baking process, our sweet rolls have a melt-in-your-mouth texture that's loved by all. These rolls are perfect for grabbing on the go or sitting down to enjoy every bite. Amazing for sandwiches, kid-friendly snacks, desserts, and as a compliment to any meal. Soft, fluffy, and sweet.
